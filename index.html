What We Do

Services That Actually Transform Your Outdoor Space

From total garden overhauls to ongoing maintenance, we've got the skills and local knowledge to get it right.

Garden Restoration & Repair

Got a garden that's been through the wars — pets, kids, storms, or just plain neglect? We strip it back, fix the soil, and rebuild your garden from the ground up so it actually thrives.

Lawn Installation & Repair

From patchy, bare dirt to a lush green lawn that feels good underfoot. We prepare the soil properly, lay quality turf suited to Geelong's conditions, and make sure it establishes well.

Garden Design & Planting

We design planting schemes that work with your lifestyle, not against it — low-maintenance natives, colourful cottage gardens, or something in between. Every design considers Newtown's local climate and your specific soil type.

Soil & Drainage Solutions

Displaced soil, waterlogged patches, and poor drainage wreak havoc on Geelong gardens, especially through winter. We regrade, install drainage, and rebuild your soil profile so water goes where it should.

Ongoing Lawn & Garden Maintenance

Once your garden looks incredible, we help you keep it that way with regular mowing, edging, pruning, and seasonal care. Flexible schedules that fit around your life — fortnightly, monthly, or as needed.
